Our Story

Founded in 2008, Chaithanya Grameena Abhivruddhi Educational Trust (CGAET) has been dedicated to the holistic development of rural communities in Karnataka. We believe in empowering individuals through education, healthcare, and skill development.

Our journey began in Devalapura village with a small health camp and has since grown to impact over 25 villages across the region.

Our approach combines traditional wisdom with modern techniques to create sustainable change.
